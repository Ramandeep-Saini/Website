My Name is Ramandeep Saini

Hey there! I’m a 14-year-old from Brampton, Ontario who's passionate about writing, artificial intelligence (AI), the Internet of Things (IoT), longevity, and visual arts. I love writing about technology for online publications, such as the Data Driven Investor.

I am continuously improving my coding skills, and for this year’s Microsoft AI challenge, my team and I are building a neural network to prevent outbreaks of Malaria. We've actually made this into a company idea (the company is called Prohibio Health), and the one-pager can be found here, while the article explaining it can be found here. In addition, I recently developed my first project that uses AI, a gesture detection neural network. Check it out in my portfolio!

Another passion I developed in the last couple of months is researching longevity. In my portfolio, I've included a science report on how senescent cells effect the aging process. It's definitely worth a read! On the side, I’m also an art blogger! That's a wrap on me, thanks for your interest in my work!

Current Projects

Prohibio Health

I'm currently developing a moonshot company called Prohibio Health with my co-founders. The focus of the company is to prevent outbreaks of insect-borne diseases, like Malaria, by simulating the disease transmission factors (e.g. insect breeding and migration).

Coding Tutorials

A project I've been working on for a while now is developing an online platform that features free coding tutorials, to encourage kids to learn to code. The website and tutorials are currently in the development stage, but will be released soon! Stay tuned for updates!

Art Blog

My art blog is a project that I run year round, and I try to post weekly on it. It's a platform for me to share my thoughts and opinions on various global issues, such as climate change and misogyny.

Sign Language Translator

Recently, I developed a gesture detection neural network. I realized that this project could easily be adapted to become a sign language translator. To make that dream a reality, I'm going to learn sign language, and tweak my project a bit to become the translator. Stay tuned for updates!

AI Projects

I've become very interested in creating AI projects myself, and I'm aiming to create another project very soon. After I've completed that, I'll write an article explaining it, and create a video for the same purpose. Keep a look out for both!

Next Learning Focuses

Over the last little while, I've been looking into the Internet of Things (IoT), and longevity. I believe after I've created enough AI projects to bore even myself, I'll move on to learning more about IoT, and it's intersections with AI. Then, I'll focus on longevity. I've actually already begun to do research on longevity, and have written a science report on how senescent cells can impact aging. Check it out!
